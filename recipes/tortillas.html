Beef Tortillas

Description

As inauthentic as they are tasty, this is a simple meal that requires minimal cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Olive oil
  • Tortillas, any variety
  • Salsa
  • Taco powder
  • Lettuce
  • Tex-Mex cheese
  • 1/2 White onion
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Begin by heating up some olive oil in a large pan, and adding chopped onion once the oil is heated up.
  2. Up the heat to medium-high, add the ground beef, and break it into fine chunks.
  3. After ten minutes of cooking, add the taco powder along with either an 1/8 or a 1/4 cup of water.
  4. Mix well, and cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
  5. Finally, serve alongside salsa and your topping of choice.
