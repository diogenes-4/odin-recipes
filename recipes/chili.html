Chili Recipe

Description

This chili dish, cooked slowly over a low heat, is perfect for a warm meal after a long day of work, with just enough spice to provide a satisfying kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 cup brown basmati rice
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 small can of tomato sauce
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • and a sprinkling of garlic and texmex cheese!

Steps

  1. Cook the rice per your package instructions beforehand in order to save work later; it can be safely ignored as it cooks.
  2. Begin by slicing your onion and green pepper, and cooking the onion in a large pot on medium-high heat.
  3. After several minutes, add the ground beef to the pot and cook for around 10 minutes on a similar heat.
  4. Next, add the beans and green pepper
  5. Three minutes later, add the rice, tomato sauce, and various spices, mixing all ingredients together well.
  6. Next, turn heat down to the lowest setting and leave the pot with the lid on for at least 30 minutes, although more won't hurt.
  7. Finally, when you're ready to serve the meal sprinkly on cheese on your plate so that the cheese will melt as the chili cools.
  8. Now, you can enjoy your meal!
