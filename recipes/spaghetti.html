Spaghetti

Description

Simply but tasty, this classic Italian dish is always sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or linguine noodles
  • 1 jar of Classico Sweet Basil Marinara pasta sauce
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 white onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Begin by heating up some olive oil in a large pan, and adding chopped onion once the oil is heated up.
  2. Up the heat to medium-high, add the ground beef, and break it into fine chunks.
  3. Around this time, prepare the spaghetti noodles as per the package instructions.
  4. Once the spaghetti is mostly cooked, add the pasta sauce and sprinkle the Italian seasoning on, optionally adding a small amount of water for a more watery consistancy.
  5. Five minutes after adding the sauce, the meal will be ready to serve alongside parmesan cheese.
